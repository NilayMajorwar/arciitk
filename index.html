About

The Academics and Research Cell of the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur is a cell directly placed under the Student's Gymkhana that aims to foster all need related to academics and researh for the campus dwellers.

Goals

Policy Changes

The Cell aims at providing the required guidance to the students aiming acedmia as their future option. We also provide guidance regarding planing ones academics or other related issues.

Guidance

Academics and Research Cell acts as an interface between the Institute policy makers and the students on matters related to academic research and foreign collaborations.

International Collaborations

We work in a close association with the Office of International Collaborations to improve on collaborations of the institute with its foreign counterparts.
