Details for Abstract Submission


Abstracts are invited for Oral presentations and Poster presentations. All papers must be original. Any form of plagiarism or copyright infringement would lead to instant rejection.

The word limit for abstracts is 300 words. You may either submit your abstracts here or at submissions@arciitk.in, but it is recommended that you use the second option and e-mail to submissions@arciitk.in. The subject of the email should be [SRC'19] Abstract Submission - [Name] [College]. Each entry should contain the contact details (mobile number, address, and organization), topic & title, the form of presentation and the abstract.

Also, there is an INR 200 Registration fee and an INR 200 Security charge (refundable) for participating in the conference. After you submit your abstract, they will be reviewed, upon successful completion of which, you will be sent a confirmation email containing your username and password for the registration portal. The payment would take place in the registration portal itself. Hence, we urge you to submit your correct email id's if you use Easychair for submission. Entries will be considered as accepted only after abstract acceptance and payment of registration charges. The Security fees will be refunded after you successfully present your paper. The best presenters will have a chance to win certificates and exciting prizes and goodies!
