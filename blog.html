International Relations

International Relations(IR) wing of Academics and Research cell is a student organisation dedicated to work in close association with the Associate Dean, International Relations(ADIR) and the Office of International Relations(OIR), IIT Kanpur to improve collaborations of the Institute and its foreign counterparts . It also acts as a complementary link between the student community and the office.

Goals

Exchange programmes:

To increase the opportunities for semester exchanges available for the students of IITK.

Career Opportunities in Academia:

To increase the awareness amongst students of the opportunities available and the number of such opportunities through collaborative mechanisms with other Institutes.

Contact body for foreign students on campus:

To serve as a student point of contact for the foreign students on campus for their programme related queries/clarifications.
